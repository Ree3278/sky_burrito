## 💡 Key Takeaways

### Why Are Only 20 Routes Viable?
**Answer:** The other 112 routes fail one of two critical filters:
1. **Time delta too low** (< 120 seconds saved)
   - 55 routes don't save enough time to justify drone infrastructure
   - Very short routes even show ground delivery is FASTER
   
2. **Demand too low** (< 100,000 restaurant × residential units)
   - 8 routes have insufficient order volume
   - Not enough demand to support dedicated infrastructure

### Which Routes Should Be Deployed First?
**Answer:** The 20 viable routes in order of composite score:
- **Top 5 Routes** (Hub 11↔9, Hub 10↔6, Hub 1↔9): Start here ($7.00-7.45 saved)
- **Middle Tier** (Routes 6-15): Deploy in months 2-3 ($5.70-6.70 saved)
- **Bottom Tier** (Routes 16-20): Deploy by month 3 ($4.59-5.87 saved)

### What About the Other 112 Routes?
**Answer:** Never deploy them because:
- They fail the time delta filter by significant margins
- Some routes show ground delivery is actually faster
- Better ROI to focus on the proven 20 routes
- Reevaluate only if: demand grows 2×, Uber rates increase, or drone costs drop

### Which Hubs Are Most Important?
**Answer:** Core downtown hubs:
- **Hub 1, 6, 9, 10, 11**: Appear in most viable routes
- **Hub 2, 3, 7**: Secondary connections
- **Hub 8, 12**: Isolated (no viable routes) - use ground delivery only
